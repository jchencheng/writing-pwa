**📺 雅思写作：广告 (Advertising) 核心语料练习**

#### **第一部分：广告的积极影响 (Positives of Advertising)**

1. **In the realm of contemporary commerce, advertising is a \_\_\_\_\_\_ \_\_\_\_\_\_ of business.**  
   (在当代商业领域，广告是业务的**关键部分**。)  
2. **Advertisements \_\_\_\_\_\_ customers \_\_\_\_\_\_ the products and services that are available.**  
   (广告让消费者**了解**现有的产品和服务。)  
3. **Advertising helps to \_\_\_\_\_\_ \_\_\_\_\_\_ and \_\_\_\_\_\_ the economy.**  
   (广告有助于**增加销量**并**刺激**经济。)  
4. **Furthermore, the advertising sector is a \_\_\_\_\_\_ \_\_\_\_\_\_ that provides \_\_\_\_\_\_ \_\_\_\_\_\_ for many people.**  
   (此外，广告业是一个**创意产业**，它为许多人提供了**就业机会**。)  
5. **Without advertising, \_\_\_\_\_\_ \_\_\_\_\_\_ would rise, and many free services, such as commercial news websites, would not be \_\_\_\_\_\_ .**  
   (如果没有广告，**失业率**会上升，许多免费服务如商业新闻网站将无法**维持生存**。)

#### **第二部分：广告的消极影响 (Negatives of Advertising)**

6. **Critics argue that advertising \_\_\_\_\_\_ people to buy things they do not really need by creating \_\_\_\_\_\_ \_\_\_\_\_\_ .**  
   (批评者认为广告通过创造**虚假/人为需求**来**操纵**人们购买不需要的东西。)  
7. **Many adverts \_\_\_\_\_\_ \_\_\_\_\_\_ \_\_\_\_\_\_ by showing images of a "perfect" lifestyle that is often unattainable.**  
   (许多广告通过展示难以企及的“完美”生活方式来**兜售梦想**。)  
8. **As a result, people may feel \_\_\_\_\_\_ if they cannot afford the \_\_\_\_\_\_ \_\_\_\_\_\_ they see on screen.**  
   (结果，如果人们买不起屏幕上看到的**消费品**，他们可能会感到**不满足/沮丧**。)

#### **第三部分：对儿童的影响 (Advertising and Children)**

9. **Children are particularly \_\_\_\_\_\_ to the \_\_\_\_\_\_ \_\_\_\_\_\_ of persuasive advertising.**  
   (儿童特别容易受到说服性广告的**负面影响**，因为他们比较**脆弱**。)  
10. **Advertisers often target children with \_\_\_\_\_\_ \_\_\_\_\_\_ and clever packaging.**  
    (广告商经常利用**鲜艳的颜色**和巧妙的包装来针对儿童。)  
11. **This leads to "pester power," where children \_\_\_\_\_\_ their parents to buy products that may be \_\_\_\_\_\_ \_\_\_\_\_\_ their health.**  
    (这导致了“纠缠力量”，即孩子们**纠缠**父母购买可能对健康**有害**的产品。)

#### **第四部分：政府监管建议 (Government Regulation)**

12. **To \_\_\_\_\_\_ the negative effects, the government should \_\_\_\_\_\_ \_\_\_\_\_\_ \_\_\_\_\_\_ on advertising aimed at children.**  
    (为了**减轻**负面影响，政府应当对针对儿童的广告**施加严格限制**。)  
13. **For instance, the advertising of \_\_\_\_\_\_ \_\_\_\_\_\_ during children's TV programmes should be \_\_\_\_\_\_ \_\_\_\_\_\_ .**  
    (例如，在儿童节目期间播放**垃圾食品**广告应当被**严格禁止**。)

**✅ 答案汇编 (Answer Key)**

1. **key part** (关键部分)  
2. **inform... about** (通知/告知)  
3. **increase sales / stimulate** (增加销量 / 刺激)  
4. **creative industry / employment opportunities** (创意产业 / 就业机会)  
5. **unemployment / viable** (失业 / 可行、能生存的)  
6. **manipulate / artificial needs** (操纵 / 人为需求)  
7. **sell dreams** (兜售梦想)  
8. **dissatisfied / consumer goods** (不满足的 / 消费品)  
9. **vulnerable / negative impacts** (脆弱的 / 负面影响)  
10. **bright colours** (鲜艳的颜色)  
11. **pester / detrimental to** (纠缠 / 对...有害)  
12. **mitigate / impose strict restrictions** (减轻 / 施加严格限制)  
13. **junk food / strictly prohibited** (垃圾食品 / 严格禁止)
